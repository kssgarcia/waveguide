CHAPTER 2: Linear Algebra and Linear Calculus
---------------------------------------------

chol
----
Cholesky decomposition of a symmetric and positive-definite matrix
by column or row

det_33c
-------
Determinant of a 3x3 complex matrix

det_44c
-------
Determinant of a 4x4 complex matrix

det_hess
--------
Determinant of a lower or upper triangular 
Hessenberg matrix.

gram_sc
--------
Gram--Schmidt orthogonalization of a set of vectors

inv_l
-----
Inverse of an L matrix

inv_u
-----
Inverse of a U matrix

ldu
----
Doolittle LDU decomposition of a symmetric matrix

lu_c
----
Crout decomposition of a matrix, determinant and inverse

lu_d
---- 
Doolittle decomposition of a matrix, determinant and inverse

lu_d_t
------
Doolittle decomposition of a tridiagonal matrix

qr_dec
-------
QR decomposition of a matrix by three methods:
Gram--Schmidt, rotation, reflection.
